Kanji Stroke Order Generator

How to use:

  • Paste in a Kanji
  • Or add ?kanji=kanji to the URL
  • Or add ?kanji=kanji&dl=1 to the URL to straight download the kanji after it's generated
  • Use the generate-diagrams.js to mass generate and download kanji stroke order diagrams

Based off the stroke order diagram generator on Jisho.org but with thicker lines, which in turn uses data from KanjiVG to generate diagrams
